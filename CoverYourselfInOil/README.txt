CoverYourselfInOil v1.0

------------

This is a little plugin that adds the "Cover Yourself In Oil" meme to Minecraft!
Crafting recipes:
	Oil:
		Water Bottle (Potion) + Yellow Dye
		- When drinked while it's raining, launches the drinker upwards.
	
	Vegetable Oil:
		Water Bottle (Potion) + Yellow Dye + Carrot
		- When drinked while it's raining, allows the drinker to fly for 4 seconds.
